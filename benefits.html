Benefits of MuteMotion

Creating Value for Everyone

MuteMotion's directional siren technology delivers significant benefits to patients, healthcare workers, and communities.

Reduced Patient Stress

Lower noise levels reduce physiological stress responses that can worsen medical conditions and improve patient comfort during transport.

Faster Emergency Response

Targeted sirens remain highly effective at clearing traffic, saving 1-4 minutes in urban settings without compromising patient comfort.

Worker Well-being

Protects EMS personnel from hearing damage due to continuous siren exposure and creates a better communication environment.

Sound Pollution Reduction

Focused sound beams minimize the urban noise footprint, reducing disturbance to neighborhoods, especially during night responses.

Better Patient Outcomes

Research shows that siren noise can have significant negative effects on patients during emergency transport. MuteMotion addresses these concerns directly.

Heart Rate Decrease

Stress Hormone Elevation

By minimizing interior noise, MuteMotion helps maintain stable vital signs, reduces anxiety, and potentially improves outcomes for sensitive patients.

Improving the EMS Workspace

Ambulance crews face numerous challenges providing care in a mobile environment. Excessive noise makes their job even more difficult.

Hearing Protection

Reduces risk of occupational hearing damage from continuous exposure to high-decibel sirens

Better Communication

Enables clearer communication between EMS staff and with patients during transport

Improved Assessment

Makes it easier to auscultate lung sounds, heart tones, and take accurate vital signs

Reduced Decision Fatigue

Eliminates the difficult choice between using sirens or protecting patient comfort
