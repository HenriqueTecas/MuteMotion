The Problem We're Solving

Traditional ambulance sirens create a critical dilemma for emergency medical responders.

Harmful Noise Levels

Traditional ambulance sirens exceed 120 decibels—comparable to jet engines or thunder—far beyond safety guidelines established by OSHA and WHO.

Patient Distress

Research shows siren noise increases patients' heart rates by ~30% and blood pressure by 14 mmHg, triggering stress hormones that can worsen conditions.

Silenced Sirens

1 in 7 EMS personnel report silencing sirens during critical care to avoid distressing patients, potentially compromising traffic clearing efficiency.

A Global Challenge

While EMS providers worldwide are moving toward more judicious siren use, the fundamental technology hasn't changed—creating an impossible choice between patient comfort and rapid response.

Modern research confirms that in the vast majority of cases, the stress caused by sirens can negate the small benefit of time saved or even worsen outcomes.

Our Innovative Solution

MuteMotion employs advanced parametric speaker technology to focus siren sounds outward, reducing noise inside the ambulance while ensuring traffic still clears the way.

1

Ultrasound Carrier Waves

Our parametric speaker generates ultrasonic waves beyond human hearing (above 40 kHz).

2

Audio Signal Modulation

The siren sound is embedded in these waves using advanced modulation techniques.

3

Directional Sound Beam

Air's properties cause the waves to demodulate into audible sound—but only in a precise beam direction.

4

Smart Targeting System

Computer vision and a pan-tilt mechanism automatically direct the sound beam toward nearby vehicles.

Our Technology

MuteMotion combines advanced acoustics with smart targeting to revolutionize emergency vehicle sound systems.

Key Technology Components

Parametric Speaker Array

Our custom ultrasonic emitters create a narrow sound beam that's precisely directed where needed, using air's natural properties to demodulate sound only in the target direction.

Computer Vision System

Cameras detect vehicles in real-time, allowing the system to intelligently direct siren sounds only where needed, adapting to changing traffic conditions automatically.

Pan-Tilt Mechanism

High-precision servo motors position the sound beam with 2-axis control, ensuring pinpoint accuracy when directing alerts to nearby traffic.
