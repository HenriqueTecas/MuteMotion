How MuteMotion Works

Our revolutionary approach to emergency vehicle sirens uses parametric speaker technology to create focused sound beams that alert traffic without disturbing patients.

1

Ultrasound Carrier Waves

Our parametric speaker generates ultrasonic waves beyond human hearing (typically above 40 kHz).

2

Audio Signal Modulation

The siren sound is embedded in these waves using advanced modulation techniques.

3

Self-Demodulation in Air

As the ultrasonic waves travel, air's nonlinear properties cause them to mix and regenerate the siren sound at audible frequencies — but only in a precise beam.

4

Smart Targeting System

Computer vision and a pan-tilt mechanism automatically direct the sound beam toward nearby vehicles.

The Result: Focused Sound Projection

This focused projection ensures traffic hears the warning while keeping the ambulance interior significantly quieter, preserving patient comfort and allowing better communication between medical staff.
