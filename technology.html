Parametric Speaker Technology

MuteMotion employs a parametric speaker to focus siren sounds outward, reducing noise inside the ambulance. Unlike conventional speakers that spread sound in all directions, a parametric speaker emits ultrasonic waves carrying the siren's audio signal.

1

Ultrasound Carrier

The speaker generates ultrasonic waves beyond human hearing (typically above 40 kHz).

2

Modulated Audio Signal

The siren sound is embedded in these waves using a modulation technique.

3

Self-Demodulation in Air

As the ultrasonic waves travel, air's nonlinear properties cause them to mix and regenerate the siren sound at audible frequencies — but only in a precise beam.

4

Highly Directional Sound

This focused projection ensures traffic hears the warning while the ambulance interior remains quieter.

Intelligent Vehicle Detection

Since the sound beam is highly directional, it must be pointed towards specific targets. Our system uses advanced computer vision to identify and track vehicles in the ambulance's path.

Real-time Detection

Our camera system continuously scans the environment to identify vehicles, pedestrians, and obstacles.

AI-Powered Prioritization

Advanced algorithms determine which vehicles need to be alerted first based on position and traffic flow.

Traffic Pattern Analysis

The system adapts to different road conditions and traffic patterns for optimal performance.
